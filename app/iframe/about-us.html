About Us

About Us:

Dukan Mitra is India’s Start-Up online supermarket in the grocery space. This app uses its in-house technology platform to manage a network in Indian stores that enable the business to run a fast and lean supply chain from wholesalers, retailers and manufacturers straight to customers in Maharajganj city and we covered more than 100+ towns, cities and villages that utilizes its efficient supply chain to deliver their daily basis needy products to customers every day. A majority of these products belong to the Dukan Mitra’s in-house brands.

Our Mission and Vision:

Dukan Mitra is dedicated to shop keeper to get online there products with best qualities to all customers that meet their daily needs. Dukan Mitra will help as digital e-commerce platform to connect customer to every small manufacturer (Aatm Nirbhar Bharat) directly for buying their products, leveraging leading cutting-edge technologies and industry best practices.
